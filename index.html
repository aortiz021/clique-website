About Clique

Clique is designed to revolutionize the way Tesla owners find and utilize charging stations. Our app provides detailed reviews, photos, and information about charging stations, including wait times, amenities, and more.

App Features

  • Station Search by address, city, or zip code
  • View recent photos, reviews, available chargers, wait times, nearby restrooms, and directions
  • Find preferred charging stations based on user reviews and ratings
  • Input estimated charging time to receive tailored food and activity recommendations
  • Leave reviews, photos, and discussion posts to help other Tesla owners
  • Filter search results for quick adventures, family activities, and more
  • Social features to match users based on wait times and interests

Social Features

Our social feature allows Tesla owners to connect based on shared interests and wait times. Whether you're looking to grab a coffee, go for a hike, or meet other Tesla enthusiasts, Clique makes it easy to find and connect with others.

  • Family Time: Find family-friendly activities and other families
  • Night Out: Match with other groups for a fun night out
  • Professional Connections: Network with others in your field
  • Singles: Meet new people for friendship or more
  • Fitness: Find gym buddies or hiking partners
  • Pet Owners: Connect with other pet owners for walks and playdates
